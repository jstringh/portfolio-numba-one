Hi, I'm Julie - a recent graduate of UO geography. I work as a GIS and mapping technician for the University of Oregon's Campus GIS and Mapping Program, and as an intern for the City of Eugene's Department of Urban Forestry.

I hold a bachelor of science in geography, with a focus in geographic information science, and with minors in computer science and environmental studies.

My experiences with geospatial technologies – both in the classroom and lab setting – have facilitated a genuine love for spatial problem solving and critical thinking; I am always eager to dive into new technologies and play to the strengths of both programming languages and software to create beautiful and meaningful representations of processes that shape our environment.

Things that I'm passionate about: spatial modeling & agent-based modeling • spatial analysis • front-end web development • cartography and data visulaization • urban geography • public planning & inclusive space • environmental justice • indigenous resurgence • environmental cognition • water policy • wildlife restoration paradigms

Stuff I know: Python • Java • HTML • CSS • JQUERY • Leaflet.js • D3.js • Bootstrap • MVC Desing • Web Design • Color Theory • Cartographic Design • ArcMap • ArcGIS Online • Photoshop • Illustrator • NetLogo • Tableau • Excel
